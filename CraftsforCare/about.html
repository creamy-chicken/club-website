About The Club

Crafting with purpose, care, and community.

Crafts for Care Club gives members one place to organize meetings, post updates, and share ideas that support both creativity and service.

Who We Are

A simple hub for club leaders and members

Purpose

The club uses crafts to support service projects, thoughtful gifts, and group activities that make members feel connected.

How The Site Helps

The calendar keeps track of when events happen, while the comments area gives members a place to leave reminders and ideas.

Who Uses It

Club leaders can plan meetings and workshops, and members can check dates, follow updates, and stay involved between gatherings.

How To Start

Open the Home page, choose a date, add an event, and invite members to leave comments with suggestions or planning notes.
